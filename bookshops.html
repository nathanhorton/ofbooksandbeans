As you progress further in life the finer aspects tend to become more important, and there’s nothing finer than a great book with a history of its own.

Brisbane’s CBD has a rich wealth of must visit vintage and modern bookshops, that faithfully bring community together for a wide range of live local events, helping people across the community connect with each other.

Whether it be for a few hours or a full day, Brisbane’s bookshop scene has a wealth of culture to give providing many opportunities to discover and explore on your travels through the region.
