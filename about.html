About Us.

GO TO HOME

Of Books & Beans are lovers of everything coffee and book. Our mission is to help you find new places to discover in Brisbane’s CBD that match with our passion for coffee and books.

Through our catalogue of locations you can discover new places to visit you didn’t know about before and create new memories to look back on in the years to come.
